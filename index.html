    Hi!
    I'm Declan, a game developer based in the UK.
    I have been making games for just over 6 years in my free time, and published my very first game on the Steam store towards the end of 2024.

    Having graduated from the University of Liverpool with a First Class Honours degree in Computer Science, I decided to undertake a master's degree at Manchester Metropolitan University.

    While undergoing my studies here, I also work as a part-time game developer for the university itself, focusing primarily on developing mobile games within the Unity Game Engine for external clients. Alongside my experience working as a game developer, I have also gained experience working as a project lead for a game called 'Block Party', which is currently in the process of development.


    Due to my experience working as both a game programmer and a project lead, I have vast experience working within an Agile development style. I am experienced with using software such as Jira, GitHub, Unity, and Unreal, and I love any chance I can get to push my knowledge to the boundary and learn something new.


    Feel free to have a look at some of my projects down below, and if you end up trying some of them out, you are more than welcome to reach out to me through any of my contacts if you have any questions or feedback on any of them.

    While my work produced for the university is not something I can share externally, I can showcase the MVP developed for Block Party, and the most recent build can be found on my itch page. My course is set to end by September 2025, so I would love the opportunity to join a keen team of developers with the same passion as myself.
