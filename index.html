    Hi!
    I'm Declan, a computer science graduate from the University of Liverpool.
    Over the past 5 years, I've been proactive in learning how to program and develop games within Unity. I've utilised various online resources and courses while pursuing my computer science studies.

    After a brief experience as a QA tester in a small indie studio in Liverpool, I was captivated by the team's creative energy. This experience ignited my passion for game development. I've since honed my skills in organisation, forward-thinking, and good coding practices through numerous academic and personal projects.

    Like many students finishing their degree, however, I realised I was short of one key thing: A portfolio to show my work! Over the last 6-8 months since finishing my degree, I have developed and released my game 'Blasteroids' on Steam, and nearing the end of January 2025, I will be forming a small indie team to build my second game in the hopes of entering into the Tranzfuser competition. As the game idea was my own, I will lead and manage this team throughout the development process (something I am truly excited to be a part of).


    I am now working towards attaining a master's degree in Computer Games Development with Manchester Metropolitan University, and I will graduate in 2025. Over this year, I aim to fill in some gaps in my knowledge, especially regarding skills in Unreal development, leadership, team collaboration, and GitHub version control.


    If you are interested in looking at some of my projects, you can find some of my main projects listed below and on my Itch.io page. If you have any questions, please feel free to contact me :)
