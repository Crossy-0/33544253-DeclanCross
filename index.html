    Hi!
    I'm Declan, a computer science graduate from the University of Liverpool.
    Over the course of the last 5 years, I have been learning how to program and develop games within unity through various online resources and courses while undergoing my studies.

    After working briefly as a QA tester within a small indie studio based in Liverpool, I realised what it was like to be a part of such a creative team and I fell in love with this. Having worked on a large number of projects both within university and outside of my studies, I have developed my own skills in organisation, forward thinking, and good coding practices.

    Like many students finishing their degree however, I realised I was short of one key thing: A portfolio to show my work off! Over the last 6-8 months since finishing my degree I have developed and released my game 'Blasteroids' on steam, and nearing the end of January 2025 I will be forming a small indie team to develop my second game in the hopes to enter into the Tranzfuser competition. As the game idea was my own, I will be in charge of leading and managing this team throughout the development process (something I can say I am truly excited to be a part of).


    I am now currently working towards attainting a masters degree in Computer Games Development with Manchester Metropolitan University, and I am set to graduate Sep 2025. Over this year I am aiming to fill in some gaps in my knowledge, especially when it comes to skills regarding unreal development, leadership, working with a team, and github version control.
